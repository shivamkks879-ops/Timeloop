# Privacy Policy — Time Loop Escape

_Last updated: June 2026_

Time Loop Escape ("the App", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This
policy explains what data the App collects, why it collects it, and how
it is stored.

## 1. Data we do NOT collect
Time Loop Escape is **fully offline**. We do NOT collect, transmit, or
store on any server:
- Personal information (name, email, phone).
- Contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or location data.
- Analytics, advertising identifiers, or third-party trackers.
- Any information that could identify you personally.

## 2. Data we DO store — locally on your device only
The following gameplay data is saved on-device using Android's private
`AsyncStorage` sandbox. It never leaves your device unless you opt-in to
a future cloud-save feature (currently not enabled):
- Level completion state (which levels are cleared, their grades and
  stars).
- Lifetime statistics (total playtime, deaths, echoes used, fastest
  clear time).
- Settings preferences (music/SFX on/off, haptics, one-thumb mode,
  screen-shake, colour-safe palette, selected skin).
- Unlocked skins and earned achievements.

You can wipe all of the above at any time via **Settings → Reset
Progress**.

## 3. Permissions
Time Loop Escape declares only the minimum Android permissions required
to run:
- **Vibration** — for haptic feedback (can be toggled off in Settings).
No network permissions are declared for gameplay. Emergency system
libraries may open sockets during the app’s startup for updates; no
game data is transmitted through them.

## 4. Children's privacy
The App is rated for everyone and contains no advertising, in-app
purchases, chat, or user-generated content. It is safe for players of
all ages.

## 5. Third-party services
No third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services are
integrated in this release. Should any be added in a future version,
this policy will be updated and a notice shown in-app.

## 6. Changes to this policy
Any changes will be announced in the release notes on the Google Play
Store and reflected in this document. Continued use of the App after an
update constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
