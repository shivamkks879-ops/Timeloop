## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

```
TIME LOOP ESCAPE — Command Time Itself.

You have 10 seconds.

When time runs out, the loop rewinds — and your past actions become a Time Echo, mirroring your every move. Multiple echoes can cooperate with you across the loop, holding switches, blocking sentries, or triggering plates you can no longer reach.

Every level is a self-contained temporal puzzle. Every death is a lesson. Every rewind is a chance to think smarter.

⏱  100 HANDCRAFTED LEVELS
Eight worlds. Every level was designed and solved by hand — no procedural filler, no wasted layouts. Each new puzzle introduces a fresh idea, from gravity flips and portal chambers to time rifts and the final boss, CHRONOS.

🤖 THE ROBOT AND ITS ECHOES
Play as a lonely robot trapped in a broken time-lab. Your only ally is your own past — the echoes you leave behind. Use them to solve puzzles no single character ever could.

⚡ TIGHT, RESPONSIVE CONTROLS
• Zero input delay — every jump lands where you want.
• Multi-touch friendly: hold LEFT and tap JUMP at the same time.
• One-thumb mode for commuting or lounging.
• Configurable button opacity so the game never obscures the puzzle.

🎨 PREMIUM NEON-SCI-FI PRESENTATION
• Custom Skia-rendered vector character with 8 poses and 6 unlockable skins.
• Animated parallax star field, glowing hazards, particle bursts, screen shake.
• Original synth soundtrack + tactile haptic feedback per action.

🏆 PROGRESS, MASTERY, COLLECTIONS
• Star-graded S / A / B / C rankings on every level.
• 6 unlockable robot skins tied to completion milestones.
• 10 achievements — from First Steps to Chronos Slain.
• Statistics screen: total playtime, deaths, echoes, fastest clear.

♿ ACCESSIBILITY FIRST
• Colorblind-safe palette toggle.
• Adjustable haptics and screen shake.
• Fully landscape-optimised, immersive fullscreen, edge-to-edge on notched displays.

100% OFFLINE. NO ADS DURING PLAY. NO PAY-TO-WIN.

Time Loop Escape is a self-contained premium puzzle platformer inspired by the tight, thoughtful design of games like Portal, Braid, and Limbo — rebuilt from the ground up for a mobile screen you actually enjoy holding.

Can you escape the loop?
```
